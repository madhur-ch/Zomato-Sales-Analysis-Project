# Zomato-Sales-Analysis-Project
This project analyzes Zomato food delivery data to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and restaurant trends using SQL, Power Query, and Power BI. The objective was to convert raw data into meaningful business insights by identifying top and bottom performing restaurants, analyzing monthly revenue and order trends, studying Average Order Value (AOV), understanding peak ordering hours, and segmenting customers based on spending patterns.
The analysis reveals that revenue fluctuations depend on both order volume and customer spending, Fast Food is the highest revenue-generating cuisine, and a small number of restaurants contribute a significant share of total revenue.
Through interactive dashboards, the project highlights time-based and state-wise performance, customer preferences, and operational opportunities. Overall, this project demonstrates how data can be transformed into actionable insights to improve marketing strategies, optimize peak-hour operations, increase customer spending, and support better business decision-making.
